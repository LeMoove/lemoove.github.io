My Volunteering
Basketball
Being a top-level basketball player is a source of pride and dedication. It was during those years that I really developed a love for competition. I was able to develop my leadership skills and team communication.
ESAIP Student Sports Union
As the Founder and former President of the ESAIP Student Sports Union, I successfully established a partnership with the FFSU league, enabling our school to enter the prestigious French Grandes Écoles championship. This initiative introduced competitive sports to the school, complementing the existing activities and fostering a deeper sense of teamwork and collaboration among students.

As a streamer, I have successfully built a dedicated community of followers who engage with my content regularly. I manage a team that works with me to enhance my brand and image, while also staying attuned to the nuances of social media algorithms. This allows me to optimize content, reach a broader audience, and maintain strong communication with my community.
Association
